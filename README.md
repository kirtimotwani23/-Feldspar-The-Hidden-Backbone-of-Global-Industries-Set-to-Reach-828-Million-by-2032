Feldspar is a group of rock-forming minerals that make up the majority of the Earth's crust. It is primarily composed of aluminum silicates combined with potassium, sodium, and calcium. Feldspar is widely used in industries such as glassmaking, ceramics, paints, plastics, and adhesives due to its desirable physical and chemical properties. It is found in igneous, metamorphic, and sedimentary rocks, making it one of the most abundant minerals on Earth.
